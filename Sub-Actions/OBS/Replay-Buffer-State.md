## Overview
`Start`, `Stop` or `Save` your Replay Buffer

![overview.png](/Sub-Actions/OBS/set-replay-buffer-state/overview.png =400x)

## Configuration
### State
Name | Description
----:|:------------
`Start` | Starts your Replay Buffer
`Stop` | Stops your Replay Buffer
`Save` | Saves your Replay Buffer
